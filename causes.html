Services

Assessment and counselling

Here we look at biography of the child l.e prenatal, perinatal and postnatal history in order to discover the would be possible cause of the disorder. We request the parents for their initial concerns and later draw a treatment plan for the child After identifying the condition we give counselling to the parent such that they get out of denial and also be able to support the child.

Occupational therapy

here we offer self-care, work and play activities to promote and maintain health, prevent disability, increase independent function, and enhance development. Occupation includes all the activities or tasks that a Child will perform each day. For example, learning how to dress up, playing a sport, taking a class, cooking a meal, getting together with friends to learning how to earn a living.

Donate Now

Special needs education

Children are taught in small groups under a specialised curriculum that involves a rich number of teachers and various facilities and equipment that meet the needs of these children. Such children that benefit from our setting include the following conditions: autism, down syndrome, cerebral palsy, intellectual impairment and dyslexia. We run classes ranging from kindergarten, primary and basic lower secondary education

Behavioral Management

We guide your child behaviour towards appropriate way of behaving. We draw different strategies such that children can improve their behaviours through maintaining a routine, setting rules together with children, creating stimulating lessons, using positive language and also developing a positive relationship with learners helps them to improve their behaviours hence self discipline, sense of responsibility, safe learning environment and self motivation

Basic vocational skills training

Reading writing and math skills for personal development are key at the Center for all those who are able. We also train our learners life skill activities to be able to live an independent life in future. Such training includes bakery and cookery, tailoring and design, computer literacy , crop and animal husbandry and more

Speech and language therapy

We provide life improving treatment, support and care to children with difficulties in communication,eating and swallowing.
