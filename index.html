Welcome to Special Talents Angels' Center

We are here to help everyone in need

The Genesis of this organization runs back to 2006 when one of the Directors Mrs.Nakate Juliet Mubiru had grown up with a deaf Aunt who always struggled with communication and only used vague gestures that were hard to interprete so she beared in mind that one time if given chance she would enroll for a special needs course such that other children of the same category may be helped and understood.

In 2008 she fulfilled her pledge and she was awarded a postgraduate diploma in special needs education. After the course she managed children from ordinary schools which were so scattered from each other until recently in 2015 when the two directors decided to establish a Center for children with various educational needs.

Achievements

Center Registration

Special Talents Angels' Center was finally registered in 2018 as a non Governmental organisation Reg no. MIA/ NB/2018/04/1136.

Read more

Children enrollment

The Center has been able to enroll 65 children of which 42 are boys and 23 are girls where some are residents while others commute from home.

Read more

Public awareness

We have been able to sensitize the public about Disability causes, symptoms, solutions and counselling through Radio talk shows, Television talk shows, speech days and also on some visitation days. This has helped many people to understand and also avoid possible causes of disability.We have been able to sensitize the public about Disability causes and symptoms, as well as solutions and counselling, through radio and television talk shows, speech days, and visits to various places.

Read more

Skills development

The Center equips its learners with a number of skills such that they could be of great importance and independent in their communities. Such skills include; tailoring, computer training, art and craft, shoe making, poultry and rabbit keeping, liquid soap making, yoghurt preparation and baking with cookery.
